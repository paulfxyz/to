Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

🔑

Your API key, your inbox

Messages are delivered via your own Resend account. We never read or store your messages.

⏱️

Timed canvas

An always-running timer reminds senders how long they've been composing. Focus and intentionality built in.

🎤

Voice recording

Record a voice note directly in the browser. The audio file lands in your email as a download link.

📎

File attachments

Drag and drop any file. It's uploaded securely and linked in the email — no limits on type.

🌐

10 languages

English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Chinese, Hindi, Japanese, Russian. More coming.

🌙

Dark & light mode

Follows your system preference. Manual toggle always available.

🔒

AES-256 encryption

Your Resend API key is encrypted at rest with PBKDF2 + AES-256-CBC. PIN-protected settings.

🛠️

Fully open source

MIT licensed. Fork it, self-host it, extend it. No vendor lock-in, ever.

Built in the open

howlr is MIT licensed. The full source — backend, landing, canvas — lives on GitHub. Self-host on Fly.io in minutes.
